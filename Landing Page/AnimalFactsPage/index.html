A cat has claws that come out and then retract. This gives them a weapon to use when they want to defend themselves. A cat is capable of turning their ears 180 degrees. This lets them move their ears to where the sound is located and the shape allows them to hear better. Some people believe that cats have a 6th sense which allows them to locate home from long distances away. Their ears move independently from each other which allows each ear to move to different sounds. They can also detect sounds higher than humans can hear.

A cat's tongue has something called papillae which are backwards hooks that allow the cat to scoop up meat from pray and water when they drink.
